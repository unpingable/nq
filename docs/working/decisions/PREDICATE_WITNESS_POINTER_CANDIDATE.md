## The seam

Robust aggregation (moderation, reputation, bridging, Sybil-resistance) requires
**participant-distinguishing entropy**. Privacy engineering decides *who learns*
the discriminant (public / operator / ZK-mechanism-only). Neither answers the
prior question: **who authored the coordinate system** — the cleavage, the
geometry, the category — that makes the entropy meaningful. ZK protects the
*subject* from exposure; it does nothing about the *predicate* being an authored
object. The invariant: **no authority from predicate satisfaction alone** — a
proof *satisfies* a predicate, it does not *authorize* it. `signed ≠ witnessed`,
applied to classification schemes.

## NQ's role (and the fence on active witness work)

NQ is the **witness plane**. It produces bounded testimony — *"I observed X by
method M at time T within scope S."* In this doctrine NQ's refusal is sharp and
worth holding while the witnessed-derivation / active-witness work is in flight:

> **A witness reports an observation. It does not author or authorize the
> category the observation will be sorted into.**

"The model found clusters" is a *declaration*, not a witness. Witnessing the
*cleavage-geometry itself* — establishing that a declared discriminant
corresponds to something real, fit for a stated claim — is a different and harder
act than witnessing an observation, and it must not be smuggled in under the
witness label. If NQ's witness grammar ever starts certifying *categories* rather
than *observations*, that is the laundering step the whole doctrine exists to
refuse — surface it as its own seam, do not let it ride the observation witness.

Downstream of NQ's testimony: a predicate-*declaration* (authored, scoped,
versioned), a predicate-*witness* (admissibility evidence the category is real
enough for the use), admission (Wicket), authority (Standing), spend (LA). NQ
owns the first witness link only — `declaration ≠ witness`, and `observation-
witness ≠ category-witness`.
