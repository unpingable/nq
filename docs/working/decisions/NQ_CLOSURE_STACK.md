## What the closure stack accomplishes

When all four slices are authorized and shipped:

- **Accounted** (per [JURISDICTIONAL_COMPLETENESS](JURISDICTIONAL_COMPLETENESS.md)). Every signal in the estate has a disposition: witnessed, debt-ledgered, or out-of-scope. Including the operator-shaped intervention channel (NQ-CLOSE-001).
- **Drillable** (per [JURISDICTIONAL_COMPLETENESS](JURISDICTIONAL_COMPLETENESS.md) Drill column; per existing D0-Origin pattern). Every evaluator class has scheduled staged conditions with receipts.
- **Evidence-locked** (per [OBSERVATION_PLANE_GAP](../gaps/OBSERVATION_PLANE_GAP.md)). The evidence locker stays at the scene; what crosses boundaries is testimony.
- **Forgetting-disciplined** (NQ-CLOSE-002). Evidence may expire; citations may not silently dangle.
- **Structural limits named** (NQ-CLOSE-003). The host-trust boundary, the witness's-own-Δo cell, the federation-never-acts constitutional rule — all explicit, none hidden behind crypto cosplay.
- **Federation-ready** (NQ-FED-000). Every finding minted from now on carries durable provenance, so future federation is not gated on un-provenance'd inheritance.

## Authorization protocol

Each slice is authorized independently. Authorization means:

- Operator says "do NQ-CLOSE-NNN" or equivalent;
- The gap doc's acceptance shape is reviewed and either ratified or revised at the moment of authorization;
- Candidate field names (CLI, JSON, finding-state vocabulary) get one careful review pass per `feedback_authority_effect_calibration` (consumed vocabulary class);
- Implementation proceeds against the ratified shape, not the candidate shape;
- Each slice may ship in multiple sub-versions (V1.0 / V1.1 / V1.2 pattern from prior NQ slices is fine).

The stack does not require strict-order authorization. The ranking is the operator's recommendation; deviations are operator's call. NQ-CLOSE-002's retention-windows decision is the most retrofit-sensitive — if the stack is interrupted, that decision is the one to commit to first regardless of which slice ships.
